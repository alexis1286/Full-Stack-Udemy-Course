The Last Of Us

About

The Last of Us is a post-apocalyptic action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. Released for the PlayStation 3 in 2013 and later remastered for PS4, it follows a gripping story of survival, loss, and unexpected connection in a world overrun by infection.

Gameplay

Stealth & Combat

Use crafted weapons and silent takedowns to survive hostile enemies — both infected and human.

Exploration & Story

Explore a richly detailed post-apocalyptic world, uncovering hidden stories and emotional character arcs.

Crafting System

Gather resources to create healing kits, bombs, and tools — all in real-time during high-stakes gameplay.

Meet the Characters

Joel

A hardened survivor with a troubled past. Joel is fiercely protective and forms an unbreakable bond with Ellie.

Ellie

Brave, smart, and immune to the infection, Ellie’s journey across the country reveals both the beauty and darkness of humanity.

Abby

Strong and determined, Abby’s story is one of vengeance, loss, and unexpected redemption in a broken world.
