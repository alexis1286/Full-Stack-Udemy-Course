The Last Of Us

About

The Last of Us is a post-apocalyptic action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation 3 in June 2013, and later remastered for the PlayStation 4 in July 2014.

Gameplay

Stealth & Combat

Use crafted weapons and silent takedowns to survive hostile enemies — both infected and human.

Exploration & Story

Explore a richly detailed post-apocalyptic world, uncovering hidden stories and emotional character arcs.

Crafting System

Gather resources to create healing kits, bombs, and tools — all in real-time during high-stakes gameplay.
